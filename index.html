Who We Are

Driven by Curiosity, United by Passion

Team Aeros Robotics (FTC 30454) is a competitive FIRST Tech Challenge team from Kanata, Ontario. We're dedicated to inspiring youth through robotics, fostering innovation, and building tomorrow's engineers.

Innovation
Pushing boundaries with creative solutions and cutting-edge robotics technology that challenges conventional thinking.
Teamwork
Collaborating to achieve excellence through diverse skills and perspectives, building stronger solutions together.
Engineering
Developing practical skills and real-world problem-solving abilities that prepare us for tomorrow's challenges.
Sustainability
Building an accessible and sustainable program that inspires future generations of STEM leaders.

Season Timeline

Our 2025-2026 Journey

Follow our progress through the FIRST Tech Challenge "DECODE" season as we design, build, and compete.

Phase 1 Summer 2025
Off Season Prep
Team recruitment, sponsor outreach, and strategic planning for the upcoming season.
Phase 2 Sep – Oct 2025
Prototyping
Game kickoff, robot design, and extensive prototyping. Achieved complete CAD model and Ri30h scouting.
Phase 3 Oct – Nov 2025
Robot Build & Driver Prep
Intensive driver practice, autonomous programming refinement, and competition strategy development.
Phase 4 Nov – Dec 2025
Qualifiers
Regional qualifier events. Qualified Top 5 from both qualifiers with major robot improvements.
Phase 5 Dec 2025 – Feb 2026
Finals Prep
Final optimizations, strategy refinement, and preparation for Ontario Provincial Championships.
Phase 6 February 2026
Championships
Ontario Provincial Championships for the 2025/2026 Decode Season.

About FIRST Tech Challenge

FIRST Tech Challenge (FTC) is a robotics competition for students in grades 7-12. Teams design, build, and program robots to compete in an alliance format against other teams. It's not just about robots—it's about building skills, confidence, and a passion for STEM.
